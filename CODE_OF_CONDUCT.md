# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

JoyfulHouse open-source projects exist to help people. We want participation to
be a respectful, harassment-free experience for everyone — regardless of
background, identity, or experience level.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful, considerate, and constructive.
- Welcome newcomers and assume good intent.
- Give and accept feedback gracefully.
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments.
- Personal or political attacks.
- Publishing others' private information without their consent.
- Any conduct that would reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional setting.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies in all project spaces — issues, pull requests,
discussions, and any other channels — and when an individual is representing the
project in public spaces.

## Enforcement

Maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing these standards and may
remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate them.

Report unacceptable behavior privately to **bryan.li@gmail.com**. All reports
will be reviewed and handled discreetly, and maintainers will respect the
privacy and safety of the reporter.
